Petrapixel's Advent Calendar Challenge (2025) - Week 1

This advent calendar challenge looked interesting, so I thought I'd give it a try. However, instead of doing each challenge daily, like how you are supposed to, I've decided to do the challenges weekly. As in, I will do one week's worth of challenges in one shot at the end of the week. Is that cheating? Probably, yeah, but I don't have the time to attempt the challenges during weekdays.

Also in the interest of time, I probably won't be doing the additional challenges on the list, unless there is one that I really like.

üéÅ Day 4 - Inbox Organization

Challenge We're not done with cleaning up! Your email inbox is looking messy, isn't it? Time to clean that up! You should probably create some folders while you're at it.

Bonus challenge for tryhards & go-getters:

Create some rules for incoming emails to help with organisation, like moving all mails from a particular sender into a particular folder.

I have too many email inboxes, so I'll just do this for my Serious Business inbox where I receive Very Important emails. It's unfortunately also cluttered up with random notifications.

I already have rules in place to automatically filter emails into different folders, so I went through and cleared out those folders, deleting or marking everything as read. There were also some emails that still got through the filter to my inbox, so I sorted those out and deleted whatever was not needed.

Clean inbox!

üéÅ Day 5 - Uninstalling

Challenge Today we're uninstalling EVERYTHING we don't need anymore. This means apps on your phone, games, and software on your PC.

Bonus challenge for tryhards & go-getters:

Once you're done uninstalling you can update all your apps/programs/games to make sure you're up-to-date with everything.

I already periodically uninstall large apps on my laptop that I don't need anymore to make space for new games that I want to try out. (I recently downloaded Where Winds Meet and that was 100GB!)

There were still some random apps that I don't even remember installing (like Disney+ ???) so I got rid of those.

Similarly, I keep my phone apps clean as I don't like downloading new apps in the first place, so there wasn't anything I needed to remove. I did update some games I want to pick up again, but otherwise, I won't update any app that I don't absolutely have to (because some apps ‚Äî ahem, Capcut ‚Äî actively make themselves worse every update).

Challenge Install the StayFree app to limit your time spent using certain apps. We're taking back our free time and ending the doomscrolling.

Bonus challenge for tryhards & go-getters:

Install Distract Me Not or a similar browser extension to block certain websites that you don't want to spend time on or want to stop using altogether.
