[This advent calendar challenge](https://petrapixel.neocities.org/fun/advent-calendar-2025) looked interesting, so I thought I'd give it a try. However, instead of doing each challenge daily, like how you are supposed to, I've decided to do the challenges weekly. As in, I will do one week's worth of challenges in one shot at the end of the week. Is that cheating? Probably, yeah, but I don't have the time to attempt the challenges during week days.

Also in the interest of time, I probably won't be attempting the additional challenges on the list, unless there is one that I really like.
